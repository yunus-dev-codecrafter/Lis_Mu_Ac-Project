Who We Are

Lisanun Mubeen Academy is dedicated to nurturing a lifelong connection with the Qur’an and Islamic knowledge. Our goal is to make learning accessible, structured, and spiritually enriching for students of all backgrounds.

Our Mission

To empower Muslims across the globe with the knowledge of the Qur’an, Arabic, and Islamic studies building a strong foundation of faith, good character, and practical understanding of Islam in daily life.

Our Vision

A generation of Muslims who confidently recite, understand, and live by the Qur’an and Sunnah strengthening their connection with Allah and contributing positively to their communities.

Our Values

Tawheed (Oneness of Allah): The core of all our teachings. Excellence in Learning: Structured, step-by-step knowledge delivery. Practical Application: Knowledge that transforms lives, not just theory. Accessibility: Online learning for anyone, anywhere. Discipline & Growth: Encouraging consistency, patience, and dedication.

What We Offer

Currently, Lisanun Mubeen Academy provides two main learning paths:

1. Qur’an Programs Qur’an Circle: Focused on recitation and tajweed with close teacher guidance. Hifz Circle: Structured memorization with constant revision and correction.

2. Arabic Language Program: A complete two-year course designed to help students speak, read, and understand Arabic, the language of the Qur’an. Future courses and updates will be communicated to students as the Academy expands.

How You Will Study

For Qur’an Circle & Hifz Circle:
Students choose two sessions per week: Learning Session: Teacher delivers new lessons (recitation rules, new surahs, or new memorization portions). Practice Session: Student recites and reviews previous lessons for correction and mastery.

For Arabic Language
Students attend 2–3 sessions weekly, each session lasting 1 hour. The full program spans 2 years, divided into 6 terms. Step by step, students progress from basic Arabic to understanding the Qur’an directly in its original language.
